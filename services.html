Your first line of defense against illness, promoting good health and overall well-being.

Our Primary Care services at NAS are the foundation of healthcare and serve as the first line of defense against illness and disease. We encompass a broad range of services, including preventative care, diagnosis, and treatment of common health conditions. The ultimate objective of NAS Primary Care is to promote good health, prevent illnesses, and maintain overall well-being.

  • Preventative care, diagnosis, and treatment of common health conditions.
  • Promoting good health and preventing illnesses.
  • Comprehensive care for overall well-being.

Our aim is to support you in maintaining your health and preventing illness through comprehensive and accessible care.

Advancing medical science through innovative trials and studies.

Clinical research at NAS plays a vital role in advancing medical science and improving patient care. Through clinical trials, new treatments, procedures, and devices are tested on human participants to assess their safety, efficacy, and effectiveness. The clinical research process follows a strict protocol, including ethical review, study design, participant selection, and data collection and analysis. The results provide valuable evidence for making informed decisions about the use of new treatments in clinical practice.

  • Testing new treatments, procedures, and devices through clinical trials.
  • Strict protocol including ethical review and study design.
  • Providing valuable evidence for clinical practice.

Quick, cost-effective treatment for a wide range of illnesses and injuries.

Our Urgent Care Services offer prompt, convenient, and cost-effective treatment for a wide range of illnesses and injuries, such as cuts, broken bones, flu symptoms, and more. They are staffed by experienced medical professionals and equipped with modern medical technology, ensuring that patients receive high-quality care.

  • Prompt and convenient treatment for various illnesses and injuries.
  • Staffed by experienced medical professionals.
  • Equipped with modern medical technology for high-quality care.

Comprehensive diagnostic and testing services for all your healthcare needs.

Our Full-service lab provides a wide range of diagnostic and testing services, covering everything from routine tests to specialized evaluations. These labs are equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by experienced professionals, allowing them to provide accurate and reliable results in a timely manner. Full-service labs offer a one-stop solution for all medical testing needs, making it easier and more convenient for patients to access the care they require.

  • Wide range of diagnostic and testing services.
  • State-of-the-art technology and experienced professionals.
  • Accurate and reliable results for all medical testing needs.
